# Business Insights

## Insight 1

### Finding

The West region generated the highest revenue.

### Business Impact

The West region is the company's strongest revenue contributor.

### Recommendation

Increase inventory levels and marketing investment in this region.

---

## Insight 2

### Finding

The Furniture category produced high sales but relatively low profit.

### Business Impact

Revenue does not always translate into profitability.

### Recommendation

Review supplier contracts and discount strategies for furniture products.

---

## Insight 3

### Finding

Corporate customers have the highest average order value.

### Business Impact

Corporate clients contribute significantly to revenue.

### Recommendation

Develop loyalty programs targeted at corporate customers.

---

## Insight 4

### Finding

Large discounts are associated with lower profit margins.

### Business Impact

Aggressive discounting can erode profitability.

### Recommendation

Set discount thresholds and review pricing policies.

---

## Executive Summary

The analysis identified opportunities to improve profitability by focusing on high-value customer segments, optimizing discount strategies, and strengthening high-performing regions while addressing underperforming product categories.
